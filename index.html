Hello, I am Uchendu Uchenna, a frontend developer passionate about building fast, modern, and user-friendly websites. I specialize in turning creative ideas into responsive, accessible, and visually engaging web experiences using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and frameworks like React and Tailwind CSS.


I'm currently studying computer science at the University of Port Harcourt, where I'm deepening my understanding of programming, problem-solving, and real-world application development. My journey into frontend development began with curiousity and a drive to create - and it quickly grew into a full-blown passion.


I love crafting clean interfaces, experienting with animation, and constantly learning new tools to improve my craft. My ultimate goal is to become a full-stack developer and contribute to meaningful digital products that make life better for people.
